About ONLYOFFICE™ Spreadsheet Editor

ONLYOFFICE™ Spreadsheet Editor is an online application that lets you edit your spreadsheets directly on the ONLYOFFICE™ portal.

Using ONLYOFFICE™ Spreadsheet Editor, you can perform various editing operations like in any desktop editor, print the edited spreadsheets keeping all the formatting details or download them onto your computer hard disk drive as XLSX, ODS, CSV, or HTML file.
